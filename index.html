How to use it

Daily Cleaning:

After your practice, spray Dolor Yoga Mat Spray onto the mat surface and wipe down mat with a damp cloth. Air dry before rolling or using.

Deep Cleaning:

Liberally spray Dolor Yoga Mat Spray directly onto the surface of the mat and soak for 5 minutes. Using a damp cloth, scrub the surface to remove dirt and stains. Wipe mat down with a clean cloth and air dry before rolling or using.
